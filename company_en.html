Who are we?

PranaGEO is a young Research and Development company and provides solutions for research and software development for data science projects. We are focusing in the area of modern data analysis in Software as a Service approach, with a special focus on improving the productivity of data science projects through the innovative features of our SaaS Platform. We also support our clients with Consultancy and Education in the area of data science.

Mission
