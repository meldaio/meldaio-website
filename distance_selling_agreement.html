In order to exercise the right of withdrawal, the Buyer must inform us of his/her decision to withdraw from this contract by a clear statement. The Buyer can contact us via email or our contact form on the website. To meet the withdrawal deadline, it is sufficient for the Buyer to send his/her communication concerning his/her exercise of the right of withdrawal before the withdrawal period has expired.

6. EXCEPTIONS OF THE RIGHT OF WITHDRAWAL:

There are certain exceptions to the right of withdrawal which include: (i) the supply of goods that are made to the consumer's specifications or are clearly personalized; (ii) the supply of goods which are sealed for health protection or hygiene purposes and were unsealed after delivery; (iii) the supply of goods which are, after delivery, according to their nature, inseparably mixed with other items; (iv) the supply of digital content which is not supplied on a tangible medium if the performance has begun with the consumer's prior express consent and his acknowledgment that he thereby loses his right of withdrawal.
